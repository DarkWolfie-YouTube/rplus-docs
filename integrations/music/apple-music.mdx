Request\+ integrates with Apple Music through [Cider](https://cider.sh), a third-party Apple Music desktop client that exposes an RPC bridge Request\+ can read and control.

<Info>
  Cider requires a valid Apple Music subscription to play full tracks.
</Info>

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Cider">
    Purchase and download Cider from [cider.sh](https://cider.sh) and sign in with your Apple ID. v3 or v4.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the RPC bridge (v4 ONLY)">
    In Cider, open **Settings → Connectivity** and enable **RPC** (sometimes called **WebSocket API** or **Cider Connect**). Note the port if it isn't the default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="2b. Install the Cider Plugin (v3 ONLY)" icon="b" iconType="thin" stepNumber={2}>
    Follow the video guide on how to install the Cider v3 Plugin here: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3dnW\_UvwEyA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3dnW_UvwEyA)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect from Request+">
    In Request\+, open **Settings → Music → Apple Music**, and choose your Cider version.\
    If on v4, check the Cider application!\
    On v3, make sure the plugin is enabled!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Play a track in Cider — it should appear in Request\+ within a second or two.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported actions

- Play / pause
- Skip / previous
- Seek
- Volume
- Shuffle / repeat
- Like
- Queue requested tracks

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Issues with RPC">
    Re-check Cider's RPC setting. Some Cider versions ship with RPC disabled by default after an update.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Some controls are missing">
    Cider's RPC surface varies by version. Update Cider to the latest release to maximize supported actions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
