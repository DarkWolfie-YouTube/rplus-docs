<Warning>
  SoundCloud support is **experimental**. Behavior, supported controls, and stability may change between releases.
</Warning>

Request\+ ships a local SoundCloud handler that reads track metadata and supports basic playback control. Unlike Spotify, YouTube Music, and Apple Music, there is no dedicated desktop client — Request\+ talks to SoundCloud directly.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable SoundCloud">
    In Request\+, open **Settings → Music → SoundCloud** and toggle the integration on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in with your SoundCloud account when prompted. Some actions require an authenticated session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test playback">
    Request a SoundCloud track from chat or paste a SoundCloud URL into the Request\+ search bar to verify it queues and plays.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Known limitations

- Not all playback actions (seek, shuffle, repeat) are available.
- Track metadata may be incomplete for some uploads.
- Some geo-restricted or private tracks won't resolve.

If you hit an issue, please [open a bug report](https://github.com/DarkWolfie-YouTube/requestplus/issues) so it can be tracked toward a stable release.
